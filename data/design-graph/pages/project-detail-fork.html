About this canvas

# original prompt
Design a checkout flow for Serif Coffee, a specialty roaster in Brooklyn.
Average cart: 2 bags ($80). Audience: knows what
they want, impatient with account creation.

# constraints
— Guest checkout is mandatory.
— Must support Apple Pay, Shop Pay, card.
— Editorial feel; serif type; cream palette.

# explore
One-screen vs three-step. Drawer vs dedicated page.
Wallet-first vs email-first. Subscribe-&-save upsell
(test but don't force).
