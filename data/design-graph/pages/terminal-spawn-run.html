what's happening

Five agents, five directions

Each agent reads the brief independently and picks its own lens — so the outputs actually diverge.

Every finished variant writes a self-contained file to pages/ and appends to graph-data.js. The canvas polls every 2s and pops the card in live.

Typical wall-clock: ~60s per agent, all in parallel.
