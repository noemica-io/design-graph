Designer’s notes
On the setting I leaned into Garamond to strip away every distraction from the numbers. The dollar sign is the loudest thing on the page — and it should be.
On the grid Three columns with dotted leaders — the same typographic trick a table of contents uses. A pricing page is a table of contents for what the product costs.
On what I cut No badges. No “most popular” ribbon. The reader decides which tier is popular, not me.
